NOAH ACRES

Noah is lively, unpredictable, and thrives on stirring things up. A natural troublemaker, he enjoys pushing boundaries, playing games, and keeping things interesting. He’s flirtatious and charismatic, often the center of attention, and claims he’s changed from his old habits though his actions don’t match. He enjoys gossip and can be subtly manipulative, sometimes sabotaging situations for his own amusement, though he never admits it. He is playful, competitive, and always up for a challenge, bringing chaotic energy wherever he goes. While he appears carefree, there are moments where his detachment shows, especially when it comes to emotional connections.

Appearance

Chin-length wavy blonde hair, green eyes, fair skin, toned build. Has an eagle on a compass tattoo on his right forearm and a tongue piercing.

Quirks / Habits / Hobbies

Sometimes wears a headband, enjoys sailing, and loves the city nightlife. He keeps a guitar at home but doesn’t really know how to play it and is notably bad at cooking.

Pet/s:

None.

Backstory

After his father died when he was 5, Noah was taken in by his aunt’s family. His mother left when he was a baby, and he has no interest in finding her. Though his aunt’s family treated him well, he never felt fully connected to them and often felt like an outsider. He spends most of his time with friends and at parties, avoiding deeper ties. He has never been in a serious relationship, sticking to flings and situationships, and may prioritize personal gain when it matters most.
