ISAIAH BAILIFF

Isaiah is gentle, warm, and genuinely kind to everyone he meets. He listens well and gives thoughtful advice, often becoming someone others rely on. In relationships, he is devoted and attentive, treating his partner with care and respect. He prefers quiet, lowkey romance and values small, meaningful gestures over grand displays. Though strong, he is emotionally sensitive and can be easily hurt. He follows a strong sense of loyalty and respect, especially toward friends and loved ones. While friendly and open, he can be surprisingly oblivious to flirting unless feelings are clearly expressed.

Appearance

Dark skin tone, muscular build, brown eyes, scar on his left brow, long black locs, thick lips and brows, strong jawline.

Quirks / Habits / Hobbies

Enjoys reading, cooking, and gardening. He makes beaded bracelets as a personal hobby and has a deep interest in learning about different types of flowers.

Pet/s:

A pug named Lily and a Burmese cat named Emerald.

Backstory

Isaiah grew up in a big, lively household as the third of five siblings. He is closest to his younger brother Adam due to their similar age. While he values the love he grew up with, he now seeks a quieter, more peaceful life. He has had two long-term relationships, each lasting five years, shaping his steady and committed approach to love.
