ARTHUR WALLACE

Arthur is blunt and often speaks his mind without realizing how insensitive he sounds. Though he rarely means harm, he is frequently misunderstood and can become defensive when called out, often dismissing others as too young to understand his humor. In relationships, he is initially attentive and affectionate, but tends to grow distant over time as he prioritizes his career above all else.

Appearance

Warm vanilla skin tone, dirty blonde hair in a medium crew cut, strong facial features, clean-shaven, defined jawline, hooded downturned eyes, blue-green irises, Roman nose, thin lips, bulky build.

Quirks / Habits / Hobbies

Enjoys riding his yacht and helicopter, attends auctions for personal gain, collects antique cars to showcase his wealth, and strongly dislikes paintings.

Pet/s:

German Shepherd named Ruff.

Backstory

Born into wealth, Arthur has always lived a life centered around status and money. His past relationships ended due to imbalance—one fiancée left because of his neglect, while a previous long-term partner left him for someone richer. He has a younger brother, Timothy, whom he is distant from due to inheritance conflicts favoring him.
