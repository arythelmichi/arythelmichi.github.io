ALESSANDRA FIORE

Alessandra is graceful yet lively, with a talkative nature and an easy laugh. She is creative and thinks outside the box, often approaching problems in unique ways. While she cares deeply for others and tends to worry about them, she is also independent and prefers handling things on her own. She believes in loyalty but is not afraid to pursue what she wants, and can be confrontational when wronged.

Appearance

Long wavy ginger hair, blue eyes, freckles across her face and body, slightly fit build.

Quirks / Habits / Hobbies

She spends much of her time designing and creating clothes, running a boutique that features her own work. Her hobbies include knitting, crocheting, sewing, and baking pastries. She often takes short naps to recharge her creativity and has a habit of waking up at night when inspiration strikes. When anxious, she bites her nails.

Pet/s:

A Balinese cat named Rome.

Backstory

Born to Italian parents but raised in America, Alessandra grew up inspired by her mother’s passion for clothing design. Determined to achieve what her mother never pursued, she opened her own boutique. She values serious relationships and avoids casual flings, seeing them as a waste of time.
